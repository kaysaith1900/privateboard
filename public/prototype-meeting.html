Value Investor Patterns · 10y lens 04:24

Direction's right. But here's the ten-year observation — collecting closed-loop data depends on customers actively uploading reviews. Historically, 90% of customers don't.

Greenhouse tried it. Lever tried it. Local players tried it. All defeated by collection cost. The only path is making data entry something HR wants to do.
