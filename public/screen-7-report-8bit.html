VALUE_INV Direction confirmed, but a ten-year warning: collecting closed-loop data depends on customers actively uploading. Historically, ~90% don't. Greenhouse, Lever, and local ATS players were all defeated by collection cost.

Path forward must make data entry something HR naturally wants to do, not a chore.

THE CRUX

YOU The question isn't "how do we collect" — it's "how do we make HR naturally want to enter it." A different problem entirely.

VALUE_INV Almost always at the moment a new hire is opened — "how did the last person work out?" If the product, when a new hire launches, defaults to surfacing past outcomes, HR reviews data while doing the new thing. Flywheel turns inside the natural action.
